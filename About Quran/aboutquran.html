THE IMPORTANCE OF RECITING THE QURAN DAILY AND ABOUT IT.

The Qur'an is the central religious text of Islam,
believed by Muslims to be the word of God as revealed to the Prophet Muhammad.
It consists of 114 chapters (surahs) and is divided into parts based on length.
The Qur'an is considered the most important source of Islamic doctrine and law. Recitation of the Quran regularly,
would purify one's heart from evil and jealousy,
and implement a sense of brotherhood and mercy in the believer's heart.
Achievement of inner peace is guaranteed if one recites the Quran regularly,
which would increase the love of humanity in one's heart,
