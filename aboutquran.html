The Importance of Revelation

The Qur'an is the central religious text of Islam, believed by Muslims to be the word of God. It consists of 114 chapters (surahs) and serves as a universal guide for humanity, covering aspects of spirituality, law, and morality.

Reciting the Quran daily is considered a source of immense reward and spiritual tranquility. It is a healing for the heart and a light for the soul.
